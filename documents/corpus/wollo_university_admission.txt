Wollo University Admission and Student Life

Admission Requirements:
Admission to Wollo University undergraduate programs is based on the Ethiopian University Entrance Examination (EUEE) results. Students who score above the minimum cutoff point set by the Ministry of Education are eligible for admission. The cutoff point varies by program and is determined each year based on the number of available places and the scores of applicants.

For postgraduate programs, applicants must have a relevant undergraduate degree with a minimum CGPA of 2.5 or higher. Some programs require additional entrance examinations or interviews.

Student Population:
Wollo University has more than 25,000 students enrolled in undergraduate and postgraduate programs. Students come from all regions of Ethiopia. The university also accepts a small number of international students, particularly from neighboring African countries.

Student Services:
The university provides a range of services to support student success including academic advising, career counseling, health services, and psychological support. The university has a student clinic that provides basic medical services to students and staff.

Dormitories and Cafeteria:
Wollo University provides dormitory accommodation for students who come from outside Dessie. The university has separate dormitories for male and female students. The university cafeteria provides affordable meals to students and staff.

Student Organizations:
Students at Wollo University can join various clubs and organizations including academic clubs, sports clubs, cultural clubs, and community service organizations. These organizations provide opportunities for students to develop leadership skills and engage with the community.

Sports and Recreation:
The university has sports facilities including football fields, basketball courts, volleyball courts, and a gymnasium. Students participate in inter-university sports competitions organized by the Ethiopian University Sports Federation.

Graduation:
Wollo University holds an annual graduation ceremony to celebrate the achievements of graduating students. The ceremony is attended by university officials, government representatives, families, and friends of the graduates.
