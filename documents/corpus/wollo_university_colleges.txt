Wollo University Colleges and Institutes

Wollo University has several colleges and institutes that offer undergraduate and postgraduate programs. The following are the main academic units of the university:

1. College of Natural and Computational Sciences
This college offers programs in Mathematics, Physics, Chemistry, Biology, Computer Science, Information Technology, and Statistics. The college is one of the largest in the university and produces graduates who work in education, research, and industry.

2. College of Engineering and Technology
The College of Engineering and Technology offers programs in Civil Engineering, Electrical and Computer Engineering, Mechanical Engineering, Chemical Engineering, Software Engineering, and Construction Technology and Management. The college has well-equipped laboratories and workshops.

3. College of Medicine and Health Sciences
This college trains medical doctors, nurses, midwives, pharmacists, medical laboratory technologists, and public health professionals. The college is affiliated with Dessie Referral Hospital, which serves as a teaching hospital.

4. College of Business and Economics
The College of Business and Economics offers programs in Accounting and Finance, Management, Economics, Marketing Management, and Public Administration. The college prepares students for careers in business, government, and non-governmental organizations.

5. College of Social Sciences and Humanities
This college offers programs in History, Geography, Sociology, Psychology, Journalism and Communication, English Language and Literature, and Amharic Language and Literature.

6. College of Law
The College of Law offers a Bachelor of Laws (LLB) program and trains students to become lawyers, judges, prosecutors, and legal advisors.

7. College of Agriculture
The College of Agriculture offers programs in Plant Sciences, Animal Sciences, Agricultural Economics, Natural Resource Management, and Rural Development. The college works closely with farmers and agricultural organizations in the Wollo region.

8. Institute of Technology
The Institute of Technology focuses on applied research and technology transfer to support the industrial development of Ethiopia.
