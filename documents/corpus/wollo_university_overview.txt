Wollo University Overview

Wollo University is a public university located in Dessie, the capital city of South Wollo Zone in the Amhara Region of Ethiopia. The university was established in 2007 E.C. (2013 G.C.) by the Ethiopian government as part of the national higher education expansion program.

Wollo University is one of the fastest growing universities in Ethiopia. The university has a main campus in Dessie and several satellite campuses in the surrounding areas. The university serves thousands of students from across Ethiopia and provides quality education in various fields of study.

The university's mission is to produce competent graduates who can contribute to the development of Ethiopia. Wollo University is committed to excellence in teaching, research, and community service. The university works closely with local industries and government organizations to address the development needs of the Wollo region and Ethiopia as a whole.

Wollo University has modern facilities including libraries, laboratories, computer centers, dormitories, cafeterias, and sports facilities. The university provides a conducive learning environment for both undergraduate and postgraduate students.

The university is led by a President and supported by Vice Presidents responsible for Academic Affairs, Research and Community Service, and Administration and Finance. The university has multiple colleges and institutes that offer a wide range of academic programs.
